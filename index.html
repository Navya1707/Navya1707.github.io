About

I am a Masters in Data Science student at the University of Washington, with a strong background in machine learning, AI, and software engineering. I have previously worked at Oracle as a Senior Member of Technical Staff, building high-recall diagnostic classifiers and semantic clinical knowledge graphs, and at Goldman Sachs as a Quantitative Strategist and Software Developer, where I modeled derivatives, optimized reinvestment strategies, and built scalable payment pipelines with distributed systems. I earned my Bachelor of Technology in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Ramaiah Institute of Technology, specializing in machine learning, and also completed a research internship during my undergraduate studies.

My research and professional interests lie in ML/DL, natural language processing, graph modeling, probabilistic reasoning, and human-centered AI.
